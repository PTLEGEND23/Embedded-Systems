>>LCD stands for Liquid Crystal Display...

>>LCD Wiring
For Arduino Uno:
LCD GND → Arduino GND
LCD VCC → Arduino 5V
LCD SDA → Arduino A4
LCD SCL → Arduino A5

>>A4 → SDA (Data)
  A5 → SCL (Clock)

>>SDA=serial data SCL=serial clock
